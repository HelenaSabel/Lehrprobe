Materials

Overview of the available materials in this site.

In this website you can find:

  • Instructions on how to proceed concerning the available materials.
  • The video-recording of the lecture and the slides.
  • A brief introduction to textual criticism and an overview of the Neo-lachmann method as part of the prerequisite knowledge.
  • If you want to check your previous knowledge, you can play with interactive flashcards related to the stemmatic method or to different types of edition
  • This course involves project-based learning. There is an exercise through which consolidate and put into practise your newly acquired knowledge.
  • You can evaluate the acquired knowledge through a specific rubric.
  • Bibliographic list that includes links to different tools and resources related to editing.
  • If you want to reuse any of the content of this website (including the images), you can do so respecting the licenses explained in the credit acknowledgements.
