Instructions

Overview of the contents of this site with the instructions on how to proceed

In order to adapt to different learning paces and to facilitate self-direct learning, course materials are offered in a e-learning format enabling you to study the content flexibly and asynchronously. How to proceed:

  1. Read the contents of Prerequisite knowledge to brush up your knowledge on traditional textual criticism methods. If you want to check your previous knowledge, you can play with interactive flashcards related to the stemmatic method or to different types of edition.
  2. Watch the video-lecture (see the slides here if needed).
  3. This course involves project-based learning. There is an exercise through which consolidate and put into practise your newly acquired knowledge. In the Bibliography section you have resources that can help you during the development of this task.
  4. After doing the practise, appraise what you have learned by filling in a self-assessment rubric.
  5. If you want to reuse any of the content of this website (including the images), you can do so respecting the licenses explained in the credit acknowledgements.
